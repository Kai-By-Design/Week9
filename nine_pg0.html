Sammy's House
of the
HomeMade Sandwich

Rocking an ancient charcuterie moniker that truly serves as a harbinger of things to come, Sammy's is a sandwich shop that worships at the altar of bovine & swine. Using old-world techniques, contemporary flourishes and a gonzo approach, Sammy's proudly celebrates its excesses. Pushing a menu that features pork drenched double burgers, a giant meatball Banh Mi, and hand-cut french fries (again) smothered in pork.
